Projects

Varun

Fishermen operating in small boats often face significant risks due to harsh marine conditions, poor visibility, and lack of reliable communication systems. ​ In many cases of accidents such as collisions, capsizing, or structural damage, timely rescue becomes difficult because the exact location and condition of the boat remain unknown. ​ To address this challenge, the proposed project VARUN (Versatile Assistive Rescue & Utility Network) introduces a cost-effective, modular safety device that functions as a blackbox for fishermen boats.

Kauda

Kauda is an innovative project designed to promote cleaner and smarter cities through technology-driven waste management. The system uses IoT-based sensors to monitor waste levels in bins and automatically notify the collection team when a bin is full. This helps reduce overflow, optimize waste collection routes, and enhance overall urban hygiene.
